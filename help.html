Welcome to the Legal Draft User Guide

1. Document Drafting

Legal Draft assists law school interns in drafting memos and letters in the correct format for legal communication. To use this feature:

  • Access the Document Drafting section on the main menu.
  • Follow the prompts to input necessary information and utilize the AI assistant for guidance.
  • Review and download the generated document in the proper legal format.

2. AI Assistant

The AI assistant provides guidance and support in legal document creation. Here's how to use it:

  • Activate the AI Assistant in the Document Drafting section.
  • Ask questions or seek assistance during the document creation process.
  • Follow the AI's suggestions and prompts to enhance your document.

3. AI Contract Evaluation Tools

Legal Draft features AI tools for evaluating contracts and providing valuable insights. Follow these steps:

  • Access the Contract Evaluation Tools section on the main menu.
  • Upload the contract for evaluation.
  • Review the insights provided by the AI regarding the contract.

4. Mask AI (New in Beta v11.023C)

Enhance your sentences by replacing uncertain or unknown words with the [MASK] tag using Mask AI:

  • Find Mask AI functionality in the Document Drafting section.
  • Replace uncertain words with [MASK] to allow the AI to intelligently fill in the blanks.
  • Review and download the polished document.

5. Database Search Functionality (New in Beta v11.023C)

Explore the new database search feature for enhanced capabilities:

  • Access the Database Search section on the main menu.
  • Follow prompts to search for relevant legal information.
  • Utilize the insights gained from the database search.

6. New Intern Task Management System (New in Beta v11.023C)

Stay organized with the new task management system designed for interns:

  • Access the Intern Task Management System on the main menu.
  • Create, track, and manage tasks related to your internship.
  • Enhance productivity and stay on top of your responsibilities.

Known Issues

Be aware of the following issues in the current version of Legal Draft:

  • PDF Upload function in the contract summarizer does not display extracted contents properly in the text input field.
  • ChatBot may not always provide accurate answers related to legal document drafting and legal questions.
